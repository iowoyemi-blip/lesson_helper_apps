Vocabulary Map

Read, then test yourself in the challenge lab.

Standard Form

Write terms from highest exponent down to lowest exponent.

Leading Coefficient

The coefficient of the first term after the polynomial is in standard form.

Degree

The greatest exponent on x in the polynomial.

Classification by Degree

Examples: constant, linear, quadratic, cubic, quartic, quintic, sextic.

Classification by Number of Terms

1 term: monomial, 2 terms: binomial, 3 terms: trinomial, 4+ terms: polynomial.
